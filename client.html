Maria is a pepper seller. She sells pepper at the popular Mile12 market in Lagos, Nigeria. She decides to take her business to the next level by showcasing her products (varieties of pepper) on Instagram. She hires a good graphic designer, who makes stunning grahic representations of her products. She takes these works to Instagram and just adds a few text to back up the images. Boom! She gets more likes and followers, and soon, she gets calls from customers who increase by the day.

An ordinary pepper seller you say? Yes, an ordinary pepper seller. Don't be surprised! Maria soon got a website for her business. She displays more of her products there. Did you say 'anything special or new about peppers'? She simply utilised the power of presentation to make the products look unique everytime. Thanks to her graphic designer! Maria further engages her customers on her website by giving them tips on how to buy, preserve, prepare and get the most out of each variety of pepper. She dishes out other related information too, such as market navigation, money management, bulk purchase and the likes. All thanks to her website manager too!

What has all these done for Maria? She gets more customers by the day! She woos her customers more by offering delivery services. They purchase and she delivers to their homes or offices. She also offers bulk purchase services and sharing among a number of customers, for those who cannot afford a great deal. These assure her customers that she is not all concerned about herself alone. She helps her customers save time and money, and relieves them of stress through the services she delivers.

Well, can you say Maria did this all by herself? A big NO! Her success stories are all thanks to her courageous self, who determined to take her business to every home; her graphic designer and website manager as well. They help Maria place herself in the right manner to her customers, giving them unique and priceless content, always.
